Types of Snakes:

Snakes are basically bifercated in 3 groups:

VENOMOUS SNAKES

Snakes which have venom glands, and when threatned can strike and can cause damage to a human body. Neurotoxic: Brain and Nerves. Hemotoxic:Muscles and pores. Cytotoxic: action on the area of the bite.

MILD-VENOMOUS SNAKES

Snankes which are venomous in nature to the prey, but not to humans. when bitten, the venom of the snake does not effect to humans.

NON-VENOMOUS SNAKES

Snakes which do not have venom gland, and are mostly shy in nature
